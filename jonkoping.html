What can you do here?

Jönköping is located at the southern end of Vättern, which is Sweden's second-biggest lake. In the summer you can go here to the beach vätterstranden, for a day out in the sun.

What are good places to go out?

As a student, your go-to club is called Akademin, or "aka" as you will know it as a student.

What are good places to eat?

Two good places to get food here in jönköping are "Bada Bing Burgers" which has great burgers and "Pizzeria Alcamo".
